Built for Greater Possibilities

PHOTON VTOL combines the vertical take-off and landing capability of a multirotor with the speed and efficiency of a fixed-wing aircraft. It is engineered to cover large project areas, operate from confined locations and carry multiple professional payloads. From high-accuracy mapping to real-time surveillance, PHOTON helps teams complete demanding missions with greater efficiency.

Vertical Take-off. Fixed-Wing Performance.

PHOTON takes off vertically, transitions smoothly into efficient forward flight and lands vertically at the mission location. No runway, launcher or complex recovery system is required. This makes the aircraft suitable for deployment in forests, agricultural fields, mining sites, infrastructure corridors and remote terrain.

Precision in Every Mission

PHOTON supports GNSS with PPK positioning for improved geospatial accuracy and consistent aerial data collection. Its autonomous mission capability enables precise take-off, flight-path execution, data capture and vertical landing with minimal operator intervention.

110 Minutes of Endurance

Stay in the air. Cover more ground. At an operating altitude of 120 metres, it can map approximately 4–5 km² in a single flight, reducing repeated launches, battery changes and overall field time.

One Aircraft. Multiple Missions.

PHOTON features a modular payload system that allows operators to configure the aircraft according to different project requirements. Payloads can be quickly interchanged, enabling a single aircraft to support mapping, inspection, monitoring and surveillance operations.

High-Resolution RGB Mapping

Capture detailed aerial imagery for orthomosaic generation, topographic mapping, digital surface models, contours and 3D reconstruction.

LiDAR Mapping

Generate dense point clouds and accurate terrain information for forestry, infrastructure, mining and complex landscape surveys.

Multispectral Monitoring

Collect multispectral data for crop-health assessment, vegetation analysis, environmental monitoring and precision agriculture.

EO/IR Surveillance

Perform day-and-night aerial monitoring, thermal inspection, security surveillance and emergency-response operations.

Oblique Mapping

Enterprise Scope

Configured dynamically to perform complex structural mapping over roads, railways, pipelines, transmission corridors, and changing topography lines.
