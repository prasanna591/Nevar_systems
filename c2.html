Built for Greater Possibilities

Nevar VTOL combines the vertical take-off and landing capability of a multirotor with the speed and efficiency of a fixed-wing aircraft[span_7](start_span)[span_7](end_span). It is engineered to cover large project areas, operate from confined locations and carry multiple professional payloads[span_8](start_span)[span_8](end_span). From high-accuracy mapping to real-time surveillance, Nevar helps teams complete demanding missions with greater efficiency[span_9](start_span)[span_9](end_span).

Vertical Take-off. Fixed-Wing Performance.

Nevar takes off vertically, transitions smoothly into efficient forward flight and lands vertically at the mission location[span_10](start_span)[span_10](end_span). No runway, launcher or complex recovery system is required[span_11](start_span)[span_11](end_span). This makes the aircraft suitable for deployment in forests, agricultural fields, mining sites, infrastructure corridors and remote terrain[span_12](start_span)[span_12](end_span).

Precision in Every Mission

Nevar supports GNSS with PPK positioning for improved geospatial accuracy and consistent aerial data collection[span_13](start_span)[span_13](end_span). Its autonomous mission capability enables precise take-off, flight-path execution, data capture and vertical landing with minimal operator intervention[span_14](start_span)[span_14](end_span).

One Aircraft. Multiple Missions.

Nevar features a modular payload system that allows operators to configure the aircraft according to different project requirements[span_15](start_span)[span_15](end_span). Payloads can be quickly interchanged, enabling a single aircraft to support mapping, inspection, monitoring and surveillance operations[span_16](start_span)[span_16](end_span).
